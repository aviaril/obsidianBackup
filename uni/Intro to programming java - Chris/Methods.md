[[Objects and Classes]]
#### Header and body 
- the header is the part that sets the return type, the name and the parameters - defines the signature 
	- e.g. Public void run() 
- the body is the rest of the method, the part that holds the code, 
	- the rest of the method 
#### getters and setters 
- getters are also known as accessors 
- setters are called mutators
