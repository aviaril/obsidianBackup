### JJ Thompson - 1897
Plum Pudding model 
 - measurements of charge and mass show that atoms contain negative particles - we call these electrons
 - these negative particles are embedded in a positively charged particle

### Bohr 
- Electrons exist in fixed shells 
 - each shell has fixed energy 
 - electromagnetic radiation is emitted of absorbed when an electron moves between shells, this radiation will have fixed energy
