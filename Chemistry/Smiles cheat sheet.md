
#### Representing Atoms####
Atomic symbols and their corresponding SMILES notations:
C methane (CH4)
N ammonia (NH3)
O water (H2O)
P phosphine (PH3)
S hydrogen sulphide (H2S)
Cl hydrogen chloride (HCl)
Normally hydrogen is not shown.
Elements must be shown in square brackets: [Au] elemental gold
