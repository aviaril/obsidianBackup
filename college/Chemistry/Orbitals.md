### General 
##### Notation 
(Shell Number){Subshell}$^{Number of electrons}$
so 1S$^2$ means an S subshell in the first shell with two electrons in it.
### S 
- Sphere shaped 
- total of 2 electrons 
### P
- Dumbbell shaped 
- Thee orbitals, one per axis 
- total of six electrons 
### D 
- total 10 electrons 
### F
- total 14 electrons
