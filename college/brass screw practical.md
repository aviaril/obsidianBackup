## Method 
1. In a fume cupboard dissolve 2.5g of brass in nitric acid 
2. neutralise the solution by adding sodium carbonate dropwise until a ppt forms 
3. transfer solution to to a 250cm$^2$ volumetric flask and make the solution up to 250cm$^3$ with distilled water   
4. add an excess of potassium iodide (10cm$^3$ 0.5 mol)/
5. titrate 25cm$^3$ of the solution with sodium thiosulphate until the solution turns to a dirty straw colour
