### Main Menu
The main menu is the menu that the game starts on; it allows the player to start a new game, continue a saved game, change the settings or close the application. It is important for this menu to be intuitive to use as it is the first impression the player will have of the game, if the menu is too confusing or poorly laid out it may lower the players expectations of the gameplay potentially worsening their experience.
I have decided to base my main menu on the rebooted HITMAN series (Sold as HITMAN: world of assassination, menu is pictured below) the gameplay of these games is completely different to my project but the menus succeed in being easily readable and unintrusive two important factors when designing UI. 
(The *options* section of HITMAN's main menu)
![[HITMAN Menu.jpg]]
As you can see the menu design is rather minimalist, using a small colour pallet of white, black, grey and red. In addition to this all of the colours used are flat, there is little to no shading resulting in very sharp lines. The red is very vibrant and is used sparingly meaning it will draw the users eye making it a very good colour to indicate the user's currently selected button. In addition to this the relatively dull background does not offer much to distract or confuse the user as may happen with busy or overly-colourful menus, this is not too say that colour in menus is bad but it does need to be used in moderation if you want it to grab the users attention.
