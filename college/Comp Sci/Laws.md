# Data Protection Act 1998 
- protects personal data from being misused 
	- Name
	- Address 
	- Date of birth 
	- Race
	- Religion 
### Exceptions 
- Domestic purposes 
	- Writing to friends and family 
	- Taking pictures for your own enjoyment 
- Law Enforcement 
- Intelligence services processing 
	- personal data processed by intelligence services (MI5) is not covered by the dpa
